## Evidence summary
Reporter shows a severe v0.12.0 regression on Qwen3-VL-2B spatial outputs versus v0.11.0, with systematic coordinate and bbox bias. Follow-up comments rule out Triton 3.4 downgrade, mm processor cache disabling, and eager mode, then show that switching from the default `mp` executor backend to `ray` restores PointBench / RefCOCO / PixmoPointsEval scores to baseline. The useful insight is that this is not just a generic visual-tower precision drift; it narrows to executor-path-specific corruption or initialization behavior in the multimodal pipeline.
