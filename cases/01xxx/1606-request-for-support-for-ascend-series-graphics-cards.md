Hello, I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to kindly request your support for the Ascend series of graphics cards in your project. As a result, many of us have turned to domestically produced alternatives, such as the Ascend series graphics cards, which have proven to be highly capable and efficient. However, the lack of support for the Ascend series in many projects, including this one, has presented a significant challenge. It would be greatly appreciated if you could consider extending your support to include these graphics cards, thereby enabling us to continue contributing to and benefiting from your project. I understand that this may require additional resources and effort on your part.
