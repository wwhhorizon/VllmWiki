## Evidence summary
The original symptom (`fused_wqa_wkv.weight_scale_inv` KeyError and ~3% MTP acceptance) came from canada-quant artifacts perturbing `mtp.0.*`, not from a confirmed mainline regression. The investigation still surfaced two useful upstream clues: `_mtp_block_is_quantized_on_disk` missed `.scale` suffixes (#43319, still open) and DSV4FP8Config lacks per-layer MoE routing for mixed trunk/MTP layouts. Those should be tracked as separate upstream items, not treated as direct closure for #43472 itself.
