## Why not promoted
Closed stale, but the report is unusually sharp: Qwen2.5-VL GPTQ kernels under tensor parallelism are both incorrect and non-deterministic, and the failure disappears under `--quantization gptq --dtype float16`. That is strong mechanism evidence, but local data still lacks a landed closure PR.

## Evidence summary
The issue isolates a TP=2 multimodal GPTQ path where both Marlin and Machete kernels produce GPU-dependent and, on A100, non-deterministic output. The fact that forcing float16 resolves it narrows this to a quantized-kernel semantic / layout issue rather than generic serving randomness.
