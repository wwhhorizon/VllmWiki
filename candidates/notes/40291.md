## Why not promoted
Closed issue with no local linked fix evidence. The report is a plausible quantized-weight allocation / initialization-lifetime problem for Gemma-4 modelopt NVFP4, but current local evidence only supports the symptom and suspected precision mismatch.

## Evidence summary
Reporter shows Gemma-4-31B NVFP4 consuming ~29 GiB during weight creation on a 5090, far above expected true NVFP4 footprint, and suspects BF16-sized allocation during `create_weights()`. That makes it a solid quant/dtype semantic clue about on-disk quantized formats vs in-memory initialization behavior, but not a landed closure.
