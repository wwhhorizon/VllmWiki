### Issue 正文摘录

### Your current environment ### 🐛 Describe the bug Brief Description Loading the 8B model through transformers versus VLLM, with identical test images and strictly the same text input (e.g., "骑自行车" / "riding a bicycle"), transformers maintains very high accuracy even when reducing dimensions from 4096 to 256. However, vectors generated by VLLM cannot achieve good distinction in similarity differentiation even at the original 4096 dimensions. While relevant recall is possible, the quality of recall is not particularly high compared to transformers—almost barely acceptable. Expected Behavior Embeddings should maintain similar discriminative power for image-text similarity as the native transformers implementation. Actual Behavior VLLM embeddings show significantly reduced similarity distinction quality, with poor separation even at full 4096 dimensions.
