## Why defer_blocked
DSA (DeepSeek-V3.2 sparse MLA) module construction corrupts CUDA RNG state — CUDA graph RNG offset tracking left active after __init__, causing subsequent RNG operations to fail with "Offset increment outside graph capture." Symptom tagged nondeterministic. PRs #33451/#34457/#34552 are mentioned (they introduced/relate to the issue, not fixes). No direct fix, no maintainer closure. Issue open, 0 comments.

## Evidence summary
Issue open. DeepseekV2MLAAttention construction activates CUDA graph RNG offset tracking. Affects standalone module construction outside vLLM model runner. Workaround: avoid RNG after DSA module construction.

## Next action
Track for a follow-up PR that properly deactivates RNG offset tracking after module construction.
